Greetings Tech Enthusiasts!!

Microsoft Technical Community is again launching a 2-month long bootcamp this year called Cognition 2.0 in which all the basic and core concepts of Web Development will be taught to you by our mentors. This event will not only enhance your skills but also prepare you for your future problems regarding the subject.

The bootcamp will start from 5th June 2023 and will include frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, Javascript, REACT.JS as well as backend technologies like NODE.JS, EXPRESSJS, DATABASE-MONGODB. All these headings will be explained in detail and their core concepts will be made clear to you.

Every Monday, Tuesday, and Wednesday there will be an online interactive session by our experts. On Thursday and Friday there will be doubt sessions for everyone and to twist things up every Saturday there will be a competition. You will get a day off on Sunday. The prices for the whole 2-month bootcamp are the following:

  • Rs 130 for MTC members
  • Rs 160 for non-MTC members

We all are looking forward to seeing you all in the bootcamp and helping you develop your skills in Web Development.
